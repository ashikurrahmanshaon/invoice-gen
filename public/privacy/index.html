Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

At Invoice-Gen, we believe that your financial details, billing records, and client relationships are strictly your business. This Privacy Policy details how our browser-native application handles information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Invoice-Gen operates under a zero-storage server architecture. When you create an invoice, enter item quantities, input business profiles, or define client names:

  • No data is transmitted to or stored on any external databases or cloud servers.
  • No account creation is required to use our application.
  • Your financial data is processed in real-time solely within your local browser sandbox.

2. Local Storage

To help you manage invoice history, client defaults, and business settings, Invoice-Gen stores configurations locally on your physical device using your web browser's localStorage sandbox.

This data remains in your control at all times. You can permanently erase all stored settings at any time by clearing your browser's site cookies and local storage data, or by using the "Reset" options inside the application.

3. Analytics & UX Metrics

To improve user experience and monitor performance, we utilize Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Microsoft Clarity. These platforms capture generalized telemetry (e.g., page views, button click actions, device types).

Strict Privacy Guardrails: Microsoft Clarity is programmatically set to mask all input fields globally. No actual text entered into form inputs (such as business names, client emails, tax rates, or totals) is ever captured or sent to Microsoft Clarity.

Your Data Belongs to You

Because PDF compilation and rendering happen fully in client-side memory, Invoice-Gen guarantees complete offline-ready privacy. Your business parameters never cross the internet network boundary.
