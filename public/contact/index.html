Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover my past invoices?

Because Invoice-Gen is a browser-native app, your history is stored locally in your browser cache. If you clear cache, switch browsers, or use private browsing, the data cannot be recovered by our team.

Does Invoice-Gen store my bank details?

No. Your financial details, client information, and tax IDs are processed entirely client-side and are never transmitted to our servers.

How much does Invoice-Gen cost?

Invoice-Gen is 100% free with no monthly subscription fees, paywalls, or limits on invoice volume.

Can I generate invoices in multiple currencies?

Yes. You can switch between USD, EUR, GBP, and custom currency codes from the Totals and Items view.

🔒 Important Privacy Note

Because all invoice details are saved locally in your browser's private storage sandbox, our team has zero access to your generated invoices. Our support agents cannot inspect, retrieve, recover, or delete your billing files. For any issues relating to specific invoices, please ensure you have downloaded your offline PDF copy.
