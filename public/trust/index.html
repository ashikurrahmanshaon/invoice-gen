Trust Center

Our commitments to user privacy, data security, and service transparency.

1. Privacy-First Browser Architecture

Invoice-Gen operates differently than traditional software platforms. Because your data is highly sensitive, we chose a decentralized, browser-native approach to computing:

  • Client-Side Engine: All PDF compilation, page rendering, and arithmetic math occur entirely inside your browser's virtual memory sandbox.
  • Zero Database Footprint: We do not provision backend SQL databases to store your business details, client logs, or past transactions.
  • Offline Safety: The application can compile and render invoices without sending a single network request containing your invoice content.

2. Local Sandbox Security

Rather than managing cloud login profiles, Invoice-Gen secures your work locally:

  • Browser Local Storage: Business profiles, client data, and invoice history records are stored directly inside your device's browser sandbox using localStorage.
  • HTTPS Protocols: The site enforces secure TLS/HTTPS encryption globally, ensuring all assets and scripts are served securely from our content servers.
  • No Personal Data Harvesting: We do not collect names, email addresses, or tax IDs. You are never forced to register a user profile.

3. Data Collection Transparency

We believe in absolute transparency about what we measure. To monitor performance and keep the tool stable, we gather non-identifying telemetry:

  • What We Collect: Page views, button clicks (e.g. downloads, stage completions), and basic hardware parameters (browser version, screen dimensions).
  • What We NEVER Collect: Financial totals, rates, customer data, business registration numbers, or bank routing values.
  • Microsoft Clarity Input Masking: Microsoft Clarity is explicitly configured with global input masking enabled. Even in session recordings, form fields appear permanently hidden to guarantee privacy.

4. Reliability & Maintenance

We deploy continuous, test-verified updates to keep the invoice generator robust across platforms:

  • Browser Compatibility: Extensively audited on Chrome, Safari, Edge, Android Chrome, and iOS Safari.
  • Production Builds: Deployed via automated pipelines verifying TypeScript validation and bundle optimizations.
  • Zero External Ads: A clean interface free of resource-heavy tracking ads.

Trust FAQ

Is my invoice uploaded to a server?

No. Your invoice is generated entirely in the client-side JavaScript engine in your browser. The PDF is created locally using jsPDF and downloaded directly to your device.

Can Invoice-Gen employees view my invoices?

No. Because we have no central database or file storage servers for invoice records, it is physically impossible for our team to inspect or access your invoices.

Do I need an account to use the application?

No. You can begin generating professional invoices immediately upon loading the site. We do not require registration or sign-ups.

Can I use Invoice-Gen for free?

Yes. Invoice-Gen is a 100% free utility with no premium restrictions, paywalls, or limits on the volume of invoices generated.
