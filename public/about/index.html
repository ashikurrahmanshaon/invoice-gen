About Invoice-Gen

A modern, privacy-first invoice generator designed for the modern independent workforce.

Our Mission

Independent professionals, freelancers, and small business owners shouldn't have to navigate paywalls, complete complex registrations, or compromise their client's privacy just to send a simple invoice.

Invoice-Gen was built to solve this. We provide a frictionless, 100% free tool to generate clean, professional invoices in less than 30 seconds.

Privacy-First Architecture

Unlike traditional invoicing platforms, Invoice-Gen does not collect, monitor, or store your business transactions on our servers. The entire application runs directly inside your web browser. All financial math, layout compilation, and PDF generation happen client-side.

Your business profile, client histories, and past invoices are securely stored in your browser's local sandbox, keeping you in full ownership of your data.

No Paywalls. No Accounts. Just Speed.

We believe the tools you rely on to run your business should be reliable and transparent. With Invoice-Gen, you get premium layouts inspired by the internet's best SaaS products, without the subscription fees.
