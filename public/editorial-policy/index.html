Editorial Policy

Our commitment to providing accurate, objective, and trustworthy financial content.

1. Our Mission

The Invoice-Gen Editorial Team is dedicated to empowering freelancers, contractors, and small business owners with the highest quality information. We believe that managing business finances should be simple, and our content reflects that philosophy by being clear, actionable, and jargon-free.

2. Accuracy & Expertise (E-E-A-T)

We strictly adhere to Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness) guidelines:

  • Expert Review: All guides and articles related to accounting, invoicing, and taxes are reviewed by industry professionals or experienced business operators before publication.
  • Fact-Checking: Every claim, template, and calculation method is rigorously fact-checked against current industry standards.
  • Regular Updates: Tax laws and business practices change. We regularly audit and update our content to ensure ongoing accuracy. All updated articles display a "Last Updated" timestamp.

3. Objectivity & Editorial Independence

Invoice-Gen maintains strict editorial independence. Our content is never influenced by advertisers, affiliates, or external sponsors. If we review or mention a third-party tool, we do so objectively, based entirely on its merit and utility to our users.

4. Use of Artificial Intelligence

While we may use AI tools to assist in researching topics, generating outlines, or proofreading, no content is published without human oversight. Every article is written, edited, and approved by a human editor to ensure nuance, empathy, and practical value that only human experience can provide.

5. Corrections & Feedback

We strive for 100% accuracy, but we acknowledge that mistakes can happen. If you spot an error in our content, we want to hear about it. Please reach out to us via our Contact page, and our editorial team will review and correct the issue promptly.
