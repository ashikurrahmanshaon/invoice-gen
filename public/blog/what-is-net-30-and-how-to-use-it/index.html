What is Net 30 and How to Use It in Billing

1. Net 30 Term Definition

Net 30 means the client must settle the invoice within 30 calendar days from the invoice date. This is common in enterprise client billing.

2. Impact on Small Businesses

Net 30 can strain freelance cash flow. Make sure you have enough cash reserves to cover your expenses during the 30-day waiting period.

3. Incentives for Early Payment

Offer a discount (e.g., 2% 10 Net 30) where the client gets a 2% discount if they pay within 10 days instead of 30.
