# Invoice-Gen.net Brand Guidelines

This document outlines the official brand identity, design system, and visual voice of Invoice-Gen.net. Consistency across these elements ensures our brand remains premium, trustworthy, and recognizable.

## 1. Brand Identity & Voice

- **Name:** Invoice-Gen.net (Always capitalized, always hyphenated).
- **Tagline:** Free Professional Invoice Generator
- **Voice & Tone:** Clean, direct, empowering, and minimal. We do not use jargon. We do not use aggressive sales language. We speak like a helpful design-focused B2B tool (e.g., Stripe, Linear).

## 2. Color Palette

Our color system is built around trust and clarity. We utilize a vibrant, digital-native primary blue alongside a sleek slate grayscale.

### Primary Colors
- **Invoice Blue:** `#155EEF`
- **Gradient Accent:** `#2E72FF` to `#155EEF`
- **Action Hover:** `#0B46C4`

### Grayscale / UI Neutrals
- **Background (App & Pages):** `#FAFCFF` (Slight cool blue tint)
- **Text Main (Headings/Body):** `#0F172A` (Slate 900)
- **Text Secondary (Subtitles/Meta):** `#475569` (Slate 600)
- **Borders & Dividers:** `#E2E8F0` (Slate 200)
- **Card Backgrounds:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)

## 3. Typography

**Inter** is the exclusive typeface for the Invoice-Gen.net brand. It is an open-source, highly legible sans-serif designed for computer screens.

- **Headings (H1/H2):** Inter Bold (700) or SemiBold (600). Tight tracking (`-0.02em`).
- **Subheadings (H3/H4):** Inter Medium (500).
- **Body Copy:** Inter Regular (400). Line height `1.6`.
- **UI Elements (Buttons/Labels):** Inter Medium (500).

## 4. Logo Usage

The Invoice-Gen.net logo represents precision, speed, and documentation.

- **Clear Space:** Always maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "I" in Invoice-Gen.net.
- **Minimum Size:** The logo should never be reproduced smaller than `24px` in height on digital screens.
- **Improper Usage:** 
  - Do not distort, stretch, or alter the proportions.
  - Do not change the colors of the logo icon.
  - Do not place the logo over busy backgrounds that impair readability.

## 5. Iconography & Spacing

- **Icons:** We use feather-style, outlined icons (stroke-width: 2px) to maintain a minimal and lightweight feel.
- **Spacing System:** Based on a 4pt/8pt grid system. Common spatial values include `8px`, `16px`, `24px`, `40px`, and `64px`.
- **Corners / Border Radius:** UI cards use `12px` or `16px` border radii. Buttons use `8px`. No sharp corners except in specific print layout components.

## 6. Marketing Asset Generation
When creating mockups, Open Graph images, or banners, **always use screenshots of the real application.** Do not use abstract, AI-generated shapes or fantasy illustrations. The product UI is the hero.
